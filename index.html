YouTube


Triggers:
Upon hovering on the thumbnail, the microinteraction begin.
Rules:
Upon hover, the cursor changes to a pointer, the thumbnail picture is replaced with the video with buttons with options to play with sounds and see closed captions. There is also a progress bar at the bottom of the video that is moving with the video
